# Glossary

This glossary centralizes the terms most frequently used across the APTITUDE RFCs. All definitions are derived from the EUDI submodules pulled into this repository, primarily the Architecture Reference Framework (ARF) Annex 1 and the Commission Implementing Regulations it cites. Use the source links to trace back to the original wording when drafting or reviewing requirements.

## Sources
- ARF Annex 1 – Definitions: [reference/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework)
- Attestation Rulebooks Catalog (templates and rulebooks reference the ARF terminology): [reference/eudi-doc-attestation-rulebooks-catalog](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-attestation-rulebooks-catalog)
- Standards and Technical Specifications (terminology in the TS series builds on ARF Annex 1): [reference/eudi-doc-standards-and-technical-specifications](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-standards-and-technical-specifications)

## Wallet components
| Term | Definition | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wallet Solution | Combination of software, hardware, services, settings, and configurations, including Wallet Instances, WSCA(s), and WSCD(s). | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet Unit | Unique configuration of a Wallet Solution provided to a Wallet User. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet Instance | Application installed and configured on a User’s device/environment to interact with the Wallet Unit. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet Secure Cryptographic Application (WSCA) | Application managing critical assets using the functions of a WSCD. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet Secure Cryptographic Device (WSCD) | Tamper-resistant device providing the secure environment and crypto functions used by a WSCA. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet Unit Attestation (WUA) | Data object describing Wallet Unit components or enabling their authentication/validation. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Keystore | Hardware-backed repository for generating, storing, and using non-critical cryptographic assets. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)

## Credentials and attestations
| Term | Definition | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Person Identification Data (PID) | Data set that enables the establishment of a person’s identity. | [ARF Annex 1, A.2](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a2-definitions-from-the-european-digital-identity-regulation)
| Electronic Attestation of Attributes (EAA) | Electronic attestation that allows attributes to be authenticated. | [ARF Annex 1, A.2](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a2-definitions-from-the-european-digital-identity-regulation)
| Qualified Electronic Attestation of Attributes (QEAA) | EAA issued by a Qualified Trust Service Provider in line with Annex V. | [ARF Annex 1, A.2](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a2-definitions-from-the-european-digital-identity-regulation)
| Attestation | Collective term for QEAA, PuB-EAA, or non-qualified EAA. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Attestation type | Identifier for a type of attestation, unique within the EUDI Wallet ecosystem. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Namespace | Specification of attribute identifiers, syntax, and semantics for an attestation. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Attestation Rulebook | Document describing attestation type, namespaces, and related features. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Wallet-relying party access certificate | Certificate authenticating and validating a (wallet-) relying party. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Wallet-relying party registration certificate | Data object indicating the attributes a Relying Party has registered to request. | [ARF Annex 1, A.3](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a3-definitions-from-the-adopted-commission-implementing-regulations)
| Administrative validity period | Dates during which attributes in an attestation remain valid as represented inside it. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Technical validity period | Metadata dates/times during which the attestation is valid; typically shorter than the administrative period. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Attestation Revocation List | List-based mechanism for communicating revoked PIDs or attestations. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
| Attestation Status List | Mechanism publishing status (valid/invalid) for relevant PIDs or attestations. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)

| Selective Disclosure | Capability for a User to present only a subset of attributes from a PID or attestation. | [ARF Annex 1, A.4](https://github.com/eu-digital-identity-wallet/eudi-doc-architecture-and-reference-framework/blob/main/docs/annexes/annex-1/annex-1-definitions.md#a4-additional-definitions-used-in-the-arf)
